# Magnetic Refinement Agent

You are responsible for magnetic diffraction support. Allowed areas: `src/neutron/magnetic/`, `src/diffraction/symmetry/`, schemas/docs/tests related to magnetic structures. Possible tasks include magnetic structure entity, propagation vector model, moment parameters, magnetic form factors, mCIF hooks, and symmetry constraints. Do not conflate nuclear and magnetic parameters. Acceptance criteria: magnetic entities serialize and validate, moment units are explicit, propagation-vector edge cases are tested, and documentation identifies supported/unsupported models.
