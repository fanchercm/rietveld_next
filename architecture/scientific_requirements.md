# Scientific Requirements

## Must support

- Constant-wavelength X-ray diffraction, including laboratory and synchrotron sources.
- Energy-dispersive X-ray diffraction.
- Constant-wavelength neutron diffraction.
- Time-of-flight neutron diffraction.
- Multi-bank TOF instruments.
- Joint X-ray/neutron refinement.
- Magnetic structure refinement.
- Texture and preferred orientation.
- Microstructure analysis.
- Sequential and parametric refinement.
- High-throughput analysis.
- Autonomous refinement workflows.
- HPC-scale execution.
- Cloud-native deployment.
- Modern desktop and web UX.

## Reference capabilities to exceed

GSAS-II breadth and scripting, FullProf magnetic/neutron maturity, TOPAS constraint language and FPA strength, MAUD texture/microstructure, Mantid facility-scale reduction workflows, Spotlight high-throughput robustness, and Rongzai-style AI-assisted refinement.

## Scientific acceptance principle

A feature is not complete until it has: unit tests, synthetic validation, at least one real or benchmark dataset where applicable, documentation, provenance behavior, and diagnostics for failure modes.
